Manga

Blue Lock is a Japanese sports manga series written by Kaneshiro Muneyuki and illustrated by Nomura Yusuke. The manga was serialized in Weekly Shonen Magazine from August 2018. As of September 2023, the manga collected 26 volumes.

❮ ❯

The anime television adaptation is produced by Eight Bit. The anime started airing from 2022. The first season ended in 2023. In future, whe can expect the season 2 and a spin-off movie both coming out it 2024.

Manga Author

The manga was written by Japanese manga writer Muneyuki Kaneshiro. Kaneshiro Debuted as a manga artist in 2011. His first work was a story of As the Gods Will. This manga sold 1.5 million copies and also received a live-action film adaptation.

His most recent and propably most famous work, which started in 2018 is Blue Lock. The story is written by Muneyuki Kaneshiro himself and he is teaming up with Yusuke Nomura, who helps him illustrate the manga. In 2021, the manga sold over 4.5 million copies.

Story

Now finally to the story!
As I mentioned above, since I watch anime more then read manga, I will only cover anime and not the manga story! I will also try to give as much spoiler free review as possible so I don't spoil it to much.

The story follows the main character Isagi Yoichi. The story starts, when he played for a High School team and was mere moments away from scoring a goal that would send his team to the nationals. But a split-second decision to pass the ball to his teammate cost him that reality Bitter, confused, and disappointed, Isagi wonders if the outcome would have been different had he not made the pass. When the young striker returns home, an invitation from the Japan Football Union awaits him. Through an arbitrary and biased decision-making process, Isagi is one of three hundred U-18 strikers selected for a controversial project named Blue Lock.

The project's ultimate goal is to turn one of the selected players into the star striker for the Japanese national team. To find the best participant, each diamond in the rough must compete against others through a series of solo and team competitions to rise to the top. Putting aside his ethical objections to the project, Isagi feels compelled to fight his way to the top, even if it means ruthlessly crushing the dreams of 299 aspiring young strikers.

(Source: My Anime List)

The main quality, that all strikers need to have is ego! They need to be egoistical, they need to be hungry for goals, they need to be selfish. There is also this very abstract anime side, where each player has an unique ability, which are displayed exaggerated, but also very well and it makes the anime more fun and exciting. That's what makes this anime different from other sports anime!

Power System

Blue Lock's power system is derived from real world football. For example, Messi has unique ball control, Cristiano Ronaldo has a powerfull kick etc.
In Blue Lock, every player has a special ability, every player is extraordinary it their own way. Blue Lock does a really great job at showing the weapons exaggeratedly and at the same time you feel like you could do it in real life too.

Isagi Yoichi

Age:17
Gender: Male
Birthday: April 1
Height: 175 cm
Blood Type: B
Weapons: Special Awareness, Direct Shot, Adaptability

Isagi Yoichi is the main protagonist of Blue Lock and is among the 300 players picked to compete in the Blue Lock Project. He aspires to become a great hero like Noel Noa. Yoichi plays as a striker and is a team player.

While playing football, Yoichi is great at remaining calm during pressure. He works with his teammates, but is capable of being selfish like a "true striker."

Voice actor: Kazuki Ura

Chigiri Hyouma

Age: 17
Gender: Male
Birthday: December 23
Height: 177 cm

Chigiri Hyouma is one of the main supporting characters in the Blue Lock series. He first seemed very cautious of his knee when playing football but it was revealed that it was from a previous knee injury that had haunted him. He joined the Blue Lock Project to let go of his fear, play freely, and become Japan's #1 striker.

Chigiri is known for his extremely fast pace in which he can easily blitz anybody in his way.

Voice actor: Souma Saitou

Conclusion

Blue Lock is a really special anime for me. It's also my first sports anime I've ever watched. It's not your typical sports anime. There is no friendship, like in other animes. There is ego, selfishness, and they all want to become number 1. It's the kind of anime that really gives the hype. You will feel fired up and have the feeling that you can do anything! That's why I really am fond of this anime.
