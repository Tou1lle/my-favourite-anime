Manga

Attack on Titan is a Japanese manga written by Hajime Isayama. It was serialized in Kodansha's monthly magazine Bessatsu Shonen Magazine from September 2009 to April 2021, with its chapters collected in 34 volumes.

❮ ❯

The anime was produced in total by 2 studios. From season 1-3 it was Wit Studio and the last season was made by MAPPA Studio.
